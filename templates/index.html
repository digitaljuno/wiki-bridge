Welcome to WikiBridge

Your guide to finding and closing knowledge gaps between English and Spanish Wikipedia.

Search Modes

Topic Search
Type any topic (e.g., "Mexican literature", "Colombian musicians"). WikiBridge searches Wikipedia for related articles, then checks which ones are missing or weak in the other language.
Category Search
Enter an exact Wikipedia category name (e.g., "Mexican writers"). This pulls every article in that category. Toggle Include subcategories to go deeper.
Article List Check
Paste specific article titles (one per line) to check them individually.

Direction

EN exists, ES missing — Finds English articles that are missing or weak in Spanish.

ES exists, EN missing — Finds Spanish articles that are missing or weak in English.

Stats Bar

Articles Found
Total articles WikiBridge checked.
Missing in ES/EN
Articles that don't exist at all in the target language.
Coverage
Percentage of articles that have a translation. Higher = better coverage.
Stubs
Articles flagged as stubs — very short, incomplete articles.
Quality Issues
Articles with sourcing problems, missing images, or other flags.

Table Columns

Title
The article name in the source language.
Views/mo
Monthly pageviews — how many people read this article. Higher views = more impact if improved.
In ES/EN
Yes = translation exists. Missing = doesn't exist. If it exists, the translated title appears below.
Depth
How complete the translation is compared to the source. Shows target size vs source size as a percentage. Red (<20%) = critically thin, Yellow (<60%) = thin, Green (>80%) = good coverage.
EN/ES Quality
Quality flags for the source article: Stub (very short), Citation needed, No image, etc.
ES/EN Quality
Quality flags for the translated article (if it exists).
Priority
A computed score (higher = edit first). 80+ = high priority, 40-79 = medium, <40 = lower.
Links
Direct links to the article in each language.

Priority Score

The priority number helps you decide what to work on first. It factors in:

  • Page views (popular articles score higher)
  • Missing translation (+50 points)
  • Stub articles (+30 points)
  • Each quality issue (+10 points)
  • Thin translations under 20% depth (+35 points)

Tips

  • Click any column header to sort the table.
  • Use Export CSV to download results for spreadsheets or Notion.
  • Start with Topic Search to explore, then use Category Search for systematic audits.
  • Articles with high priority + high views = biggest impact for your editing time.
